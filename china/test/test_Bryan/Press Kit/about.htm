About Shuttle

Established in 1983 Shuttle Inc. (Headquarter) is based in Taiwan with an extensive worldwide network of subsidiaries, resellers, partners and suppliers. Shuttle Inc. is recognized as an industry leading designer and manufacturer of small form factor (SFF) computers and accessories.

Shuttle produced the first commercially-successful small form factor (SFF) computer and continues to develop the segment with its platform defining XPC product lines. Succeeding the high reliability and quality of SFF computers, Shuttle further seeks to provide rounded solutions for a wide variety of applications and settings. For now, Shuttle Inc. has received numerous awards and accolades from independent media and analysts, affirming the superior quality of Shuttle products and services.

Innovation
Since the release of the first XPC in 2001, Shuttle has continued to innovate and improve on its industry-changing design. Now, the Integrated Cooling Engine (ICE) technology has been innovated to ICE 2, uses convection cooling to dissipate the heat away from the CPU-Zone and surrounding system components. Copper tubing – coated with Nickel to enhance tube hardness and anti-rust protection are filled with distilled water – efficiently channels heat away, providing highly-effective processor and chassis cooling. Coolest innovation on motherboard is designed onto the motherboards, using Japan-import solid capacitors with the guaranteed of maximum stability, reliability and longer system lifetime. Also, the Silent X includes power management, airflow engineering, mechanical damping and more. OASIS cooling technology is implemented in P3, delivering a unique system of heat-sinks covering the MOSFET models, the north bridge as well as the south bridge that effectively dissipates heat without the use of fans.

To boast its state-of-the-art heat dispersion technology, Shuttle launches “Fanless Solutions” in 2009 that support VIA or Intel platforms among the 3-liter desktop product line. An integrated thermal solution efficiently spreads heat out. The revolutionary fanless solution is designed of absolute silence and maintenance-free operation.

Cutting-Edge
Shuttle's determination to deliver cutting edge innovation can be seen in the motherboards that reside inside the XPC as well. New designs offer compatibility with two-slot graphics cards and provide exceptional stability through advanced four-phase electricals, not to mention the latest chipsets from long-time partners Intel, AMD, NVIDIA, ATI, VIA and SiS.
